Chinese Steamed Egg

Description

My grandma used to make this for me every morning and it slaps hard. A perfect dish to start a day.

Ingredients

  • 4 Medium Eggs
  • 1 1/4 - 1 3/4 cups water, vegetable, or chicken stock
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix water (or broth/stock), eggs, and salt in a bowl
  2. Pour the eggs through a fine mesh strainer
  3. Cover the bowl with aluminum foil
  4. Place the bowl into a steamer and steam over low heat until the eggs are set (~10-14 min)
  5. Take out and serve
